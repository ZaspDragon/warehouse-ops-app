Help

Data storage: This app saves records in your browser using localStorage.

GitHub Pages: Upload these files to your repo root and enable Pages.

Editing: Go to Records and click Edit to load a record back into the form.

Dashboard: Filters update the KPI cards and tables automatically.

Heads up: localStorage stays on the device/browser being used.
